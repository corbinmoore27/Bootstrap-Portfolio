About Me

When it comes to what makes me who I am, the best place to start is music. As a kid I always loved listening to music. As I grew I started playing instruments and understanding how to read and write it. To this day music is one of the biggest parts of my life/free time. I love playing the guitar, learning new songs, and occasionally writing them when I have time.


When I'm not doing something with music you'll probably finding me playing sports, mostly tennis, basketball and soccer, or possibly working on or riding my motorcycle.
